How to Use Focus Bubble

Getting Started

Click "Start Study Session" to begin tracking your focus time and distractions.

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Space - Pause/Resume session
  • R - Reset session
  • M - Mute/Unmute sounds
  • H - Show/Hide this help
  • T - Toggle theme

How It Works

The app tracks when you switch tabs or leave the window, counting them as distractions. Stay focused by keeping this tab active!

Data Export

Download your session data as JSON to analyze your productivity patterns.
